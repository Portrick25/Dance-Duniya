About


Nowadays everyone wants to follow their passion and everyone wants to get a chance to prove himself that they have got some talent, which they want to present to the world. Everyone deserves to get notified if something related is going around them .Dance Duniya targets the dancers who have the ability to perform well but find it difficult to get nearby audition updates. It also target those who are looking for jobs in the field of dance as a teacher, trainee or mentor. They can also get notifications about the dancing concerts /events which are being organized near them.

Dance Duniya is not just a platform for trained ones but also for those who wants to pursue their career in dancing .It also connects you the best dance classes and trainers around you. People can also apply for passes to attend concerts or events in the city. All you can think about dance, Dance Duniya brings it in front of you.
